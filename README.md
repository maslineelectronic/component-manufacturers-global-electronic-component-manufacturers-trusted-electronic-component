## What Are Electronic Component Manufacturers?

Electronic component manufacturers are companies that design, engineer, and produce electronic parts used in circuits, devices, and embedded systems. Their portfolios include active components such as integrated circuits and semiconductors, as well as passive components like capacitors, resistors, and inductors.

These manufacturers support a wide range of customers, including:

- OEM product manufacturers  
- Electronics manufacturing services (EMS) providers  
- Contract manufacturers  
- R&D and engineering firms  
- Industrial automation companies  

Organizations sourcing from electronic component manufacturers benefit from certified production processes, testing standards, and documented performance specifications.
